# Portfolio Goals

## What I'm building
A professional product design portfolio for job hunting.

## Target audience
Hiring managers and design leads at top-tier tech companies (big tech, high-growth startups).

## Quality bar
Senior-level. Every case study should feel like it belongs at a company like Google, Airbnb, Notion, Linear, etc.

## What the portfolio needs to do
1. Show I can solve real product problems, not just make things look good
2. Demonstrate strong visual craft
3. Show clear design thinking and process
4. Feel unique and personal — not templated

## Minimum deliverables
- 3 case studies
- About/bio section
- Contact
