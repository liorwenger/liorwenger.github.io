## Case Study Template
Each case study should cover:
- **The problem** — what were we solving and why did it matter?
- **My role** — what was I specifically responsible for?
- **Process** — research, ideation, iterations (show the thinking)
- **Key decisions** — what did you choose and why?
- **Outcome** — results, impact, what shipped
- **Visuals** — screens, flows, prototypes
